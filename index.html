I’m a postdoctoral researcher with Robin Ryder in CEREMADE at Université Paris Dauphine working on problems in computational statistics and phylogenetic inference.

Before coming to Dauphine, I was a postdoc at the University of Oxford’s Department of Statistics (2016–2019) and Big Data Institute (2019–2020), and a stipendiary lecturer at St Peter’s College (2019–2020).

For my DPhil, I worked with Geoff Nicholls on a novel method to infer phylogenetic trees when evolutionary traits may transfer laterally between species. The code is on GitHub.
